1.1 background

The founder believes that all human civilizations originate from rivers, such as the Tigris River, the Nile River and the Yellow River, which gave birth to the three oldest civilizations in the world. In Spanish, "San" means sacred, and "Rio" means river. The founder takes the name of the sacred river to express respect for human civilization. And then developed Sanrio, a company with social communication as its corporate purpose.

1.2 brief introduction

Sanrio is a global lifestyle brand, best known for Hello Kitty, who was born in 1974, but also has many other beloved characters such as CINNAMOROLL, POMPOMPURIN, KUROMI, POCHACCO, Hello Kitty, MY MELODY, HANGYODON, LITTLE TWIN STARS and many others.

Sanrio was founded on the principle that "a small gift can create a great friendship." Since its inception in 1960, this mission has inspired Sanrio to provide consumers in more than 130 countries and territories with high-quality goods and fun activities that create unique experiences and bring people closer to each other.

Sanrio's business scope is not only merchandise, but also touches every corner of people's lives, including a variety of media content, gaming products and even theme parks, to achieve their vision: "One World, Connecting Smiles".

2. Characters

2.1 role features

  • Uniqueness

    • Sanrio's characters have their own unique background stories and distinct personality characteristics. Based on the company philosophy of "Small Gift, Big Smile," the designers have independently developed unique patterns in the process of product planning.

  • Stability

    • Sanrio's styling patterns, through the continuous production of new products, maintain a fresh image. In addition, it is not like some modeling patterns born from movies or animations, because the movie or animation stops broadcasting, its acceptance will directly decline. They will be based on product characteristics, through a wealth of creativity, the development of high stability of the pattern.

  • Richness

    • Sanrio has more than 10,000 products on the market every year, covering all seasons and all ages to meet the different needs of customers. Their designers often develop varied patterns to meet the needs of different product development.

2.2 main characters

Helllo Kitty Hello Kitty is arguably the most famous and iconic character from Sanrio. She is a white anthropomorphic cat with a cute and simple design. Hello Kitty has become a global phenomenon and is featured on various products, ranging from clothing and accessories to home decor and stationery.
My Melody My Melody is a pink rabbit character known for her hood and her love for baking and sharing kindness. She has a sweet and gentle personality, often depicted with her best friend, a mouse named My Sweet Piano.
Kuromi Kuromi is known for her black and purple color scheme, as well as her signature skull bow. Despite her dark appearance, she has a soft spot for cute things and is often seen with her friend, My Melody. Kuromi has gained a significant following among fans who appreciate her edgy and rebellious personality.
Cinnamoroll Cinnamoroll is a white puppy with long ears resembling cinnamon rolls. He is known for his sweet and friendly nature, often depicted flying with his ears like wings.
Pompompurin Pompompurin is a golden retriever dog character who wears a brown beret and loves pudding. He is portrayed as a laid-back and friendly character, often seen relaxing and napping.
Little Twin Stars Little Twin Stars are Kiki and Lala, a pair of angelic twin siblings who live on a star-shaped cloud in the sky. They are known for their dreamy and celestial theme, with pastel colors and magical elements.
